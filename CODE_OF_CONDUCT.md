# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of the **Deep Q-Learning for Atari Pong** project pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free and inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

We aim to create an open, respectful, and collaborative environment focused on learning, experimentation, and advancing knowledge in reinforcement learning and AI.

---

## Our Standards

### Encouraged Behaviors

- **Respectful Communication** – Use inclusive and constructive language
- **Academic Integrity** – Properly cite sources and avoid plagiarism
- **Constructive Feedback** – Provide helpful and actionable suggestions
- **Collaborative Learning** – Support others and share knowledge
- **Professional Conduct** – Maintain respectful and professional interactions
- **Scientific Rigor** – Follow best practices in experimentation and reporting

---

### Unacceptable Behaviors

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments  
- Plagiarism or falsification of results  
- Personal attacks or toxic behavior  
- Sharing private information without permission  
- Any behavior inappropriate in an academic or professional setting  

---

## Project-Specific Guidelines

### For AI/ML Projects

- Ensure **reproducibility** of experiments  
- Clearly document **hyperparameters and results**  
- Acknowledge **datasets, tools, and resources**  
- Avoid misleading performance claims  
- Report both **successes and failures**  

---

### For Code Contributions

- Write **clean and readable code**  
- Include proper **documentation and comments**  
- Follow standard coding practices  
- Attribute external code or references  

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct and may take action against any behavior that violates these guidelines.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub repository interactions  
- Issues, pull requests, and discussions  
- Documentation and shared materials  
- Any public communication related to this project  

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Violations may result in:

1. **Warning** – Explanation of inappropriate behavior  
2. **Temporary restriction** – Limited interaction  
3. **Permanent ban** – Removal from the project  

---

## Academic Context

Since this is an educational project:

- Students should acknowledge all sources  
- Collaboration is encouraged, but plagiarism is not  
- Learning and experimentation are prioritized
